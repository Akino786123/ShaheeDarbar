Our Story

Where Every Meal Tells a Story

Nestled in the vibrant lanes of Baihata Chariali, Shahee Dhaba began as a humble dream — to bring the warmth of home-cooked food to every table. What started as a small kitchen has grown into a beloved gathering place for families, students, and travellers passing through Kamrup.

Our recipes are rooted in the rich culinary heritage of Muslim cuisine — slow-simmered gravies, hand-ground spices, and the smoky magic of the tandoor. We equally take pride in our Assamese traditions: fresh river fish, aromatic khar, and the soul-warming Thali.

Every dish is crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just honest food, served with genuine warmth.
